Got screens? Get Screenly.

Small cast. Big show.

Screenly is software that runs on the tiny Raspberry Pi credit card size computers for an unbelievable price. From such a small package, Screenly delivers amazing performance. It'll play flawless full 1080p HD video, render web content and more.

With Screenly and ~$35 worth of hardware, you can turn any modern TV or monitor into a vibrant digital sign. Screenly can be used for anything from displaying advertisement to live dashboards with system status and in-store infomercials.

One stop screen management.

{% img screenly-map.jpg alt:"Screenshot of Screenly Pro main map" class:"border" %}

With Screenly Pro you can manage all your screens from a feature-rich web administrator. Create playlists for a single screen or for many, group screens into easy to handle units, locate screens in need of servicing at a glance.

Installation is as easy as it gets: plug in a few cables for each new node and you'll be up and running in minutes. You won't even need to bring a keyboard to the location of your screen.

  • Fast setup of new nodes.
  • Plug and play install new nodes.
  • Nodes can run while offline.
  • Powerful organisational tools.
{% img node-status.jpg alt:"Screenly node detail" class:"border" %}

Get the big picture on your big pictures. Status Monitor

Screenly checks on every screen throughout the day to give you the latest details. View periodical screenshots of what's showing, verify uptime and connectivity - all from a single view. With so much information available, you save time over checking screens in person.

Automatic updates.

Every Screenly Pro node performs maintenance and updates automatically, making administration easier than ever before.

  • Automatic, hands-free node updates.
  • Screenshots collected from nodes.
  • Full system status dashboard.

Easy assets.

When it comes to content, it couldn't be easier: upload assets and schedule them in seconds while Screenly deals with the technical complexities such as transcoding assets for the highest playback fidelity on each screen.

Assets are distributed and cached so that nodes keep ticking even if their internet connection goes down temporarily.

{% img screenly-asset.png alt:"Screenly asset detail" class:"border" %}

Bring your own TV.

Screenly works with any modern 1080p HD TV with HDMI. Bring your own, or get one with a built-in Raspberry Pi. Learn more about our digital signage players.

Advanced scheduling.

Schedule playlists to start and end on dates and times of your choice, or drill down and do the same for individual assets.

Free Trial or just Free.

Screenly Pro offers a free trial to get you started. And if you don't mind managing your nodes by hand, there's even a Screenly Open Source Edition available for free.
